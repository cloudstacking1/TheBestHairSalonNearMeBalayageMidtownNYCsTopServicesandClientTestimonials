What is Balayage?

Balayage is a French hair coloring technique where color is hand-painted onto the hair, creating a graduated, natural-looking effect. Unlike traditional foil highlights, balayage allows for a more personalized and less maintenance-intensive result. At Balayage Midtown NYC, we tailor each balayage application to suit your hair type, skin tone, and personal style.
